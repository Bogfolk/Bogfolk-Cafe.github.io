New from the Bogfolk

The Mordant Arcana consists of two parts:

  • 79 tarot cards designed by Strega Wolf Eden van den Berg.
  • A booklet with 79 wonderful micro fiction stories and a dungeon generator tool for use in any game, written by Tessa Winters and edited by Walton Wood.

But why take our word for it?

Powerful, minimalist and magical. I want this deck.

Johan Nohr, creator of Mörk Borg

Tarot isn't magic. Or if it is, it's only because magic is ink blots. It's the incredible magic of the human ability to see stories in anything, to make meaning from symbols. The Bogfolk's Mordant Arcana straddles this line between the magical and mundane.

Joshua McCrowell, creator of His Majesty the Worm
